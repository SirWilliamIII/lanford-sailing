About the Crew

Free sailing with Capitan-Dr. Francisco Rengifo and first mate Zack Lanford! If you have ever dreamed of selling everything and sailing around the world or just want some time away from the 9-5 then you don’t want to miss this opportunity. AnaMaria is captained by Dr. Francisco Rengifo who has over 55 years of sailing experience. AnaMaria is a beautiful three cabin Beneteau Oceanis 440 which bridges the gap between a racing vessel and a liveaboard cruising vessel. AnaMaria offers multiple types of sails to suit each individuals or groups desires. AnaMaria accommodates sailors of all skill levels and may even let you be captain of the ship. There are no deposits no signup fees and free cancelations on all day sails! All sails are weather permitting.

Group Access

When sailing with AnaMaria guest will be able to enjoy one of the most premier quality built boats available on the market. Beneteau is world renowned for their incredible craftsmanship, ease of maneuverability and comfortability both on deck and below. Guest will have full access to top deck area as well as the very large and comfortable cushioned and fully enclosed cockpit which is shaded, dry and out of the weather. AnaMaria has a full wrap around leather settee and dining table area with refrigerator and freezer available for storing snacks and food of your choice. Ice chests with ice are supplied for beverages brought by the guests. All cruises have a crew member to retrieve any drinks or food stored below deck as well as facilitate any other guests needs, questions or concerns. Crew will provide soft drinks, bottled water and ice for coolers. BYOB! PLEASE NO GLASS. If your drink of choice is only supplied in glass bottles please feel free to transfer liquids into a shatterproof bottle or container prior to your arrival. AnaMaria comes equipped with all of the newest innovations of life vests, life rafts, GPS beacons, VHF radios, radar, and AIS. All for safety in case of emergency.

WHY SAIL WITH US

We aboard AnaMaria are passionate about sailing and thoroughly enjoy sharing this passion with anyone who is willing to come out for a sail. Whether it is your first time, or you are a seasoned sailor looking to get on a new to you boat, or especially, if you are just looking to come out on the water and enjoy a few beverages of your choice while sharing laughs with your partner and friends or even stranger’s/future friends, we look forward to hosting you. We at AnaMaria encourage all guests to ask any questions and interact with the crew as well as all other guests on board.

Tips

  • For safety and preservation of AnaMaria we ask that guests wear soft sole “boatshoes”, flip flops, sandals or you are welcome to go barefoot while aboard AnaMaria.
  • Don’t forget your sunscreen or if you intend on wearing a hat please make sure it fits securely as to not blow off in case of wind gusts.
  • Please pay attention to the weather forecast for the day or days you intend to sail and dress accordingly, the breeze off the bay or ocean is a refreshing burst, however, it can get a bit chilly in winter months.
  • If adventuring out on one of our anchored excursions please bring a beach towel, swimsuit or clothes that can get slightly wet.
  • If you have booked a group excursion your group will have full reign of our JL Audio Bluetooth sound system and are welcome to play any music you desire.
